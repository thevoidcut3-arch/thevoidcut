Privacy Policy

This app is a private single-user tool used exclusively by the account owner to upload original video content to their own TikTok account.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from any user. No analytics, tracking, or third party services are used.

TikTok Authorization

This app requests access to the account owner's TikTok account solely for the purpose of uploading original video content. No data is stored beyond what is required to complete the upload.
